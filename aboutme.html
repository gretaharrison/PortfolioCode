Greta Harrison

I’m a User Experience designer and former entrepreneur who makes beautiful and efficiently designed things. Some people go to business school, I founded a company. Before discovering my calling in UX Design I was running an e-commerce site that I founded in 2012. At my company I was a team of one and was responsible for all aspects of business - from web development to order fulfillment. To break free from my template-based website, I learned to code and used analytics to track users navigating through my site - this is when I discovered my love for UX. I’m passionate about functional design and with my formalized skills through GA’s UXDi immersive program, I’m ready to make my mark at a new company with a great product and awesome team.
